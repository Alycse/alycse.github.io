ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Joshua Philip Barcena.
I'm a software developer currently residing in Quezon City, Philippines.
I'm a graduate of University of Santo Tomas, where I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science.

Ever since I was a child, I have been passionate about developing software and video games.
Before I learned how to program, I would use MS Powerpoint's Action Buttons to create games that I would then distribute to my friends!
I also enjoy creating tools that automate my daily tasks, which allows me to be more productive and efficient.

I like to think both logically and creatively, and I try to be very resourceful in achieving my goals, maximizing every resource available to me in order to solve a problem. I am always eager to learn new technologies and doing stuff I've never done before (like this website, and a bunch of my other projects!)
